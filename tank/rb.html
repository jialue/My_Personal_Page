Running Back has the highest speed in the team. When it is running at its full speed, you may hardly catch it.

The aim for RB is to make it small, fast and smart, able to catch the puch and goal fast. To make it more aggressive, a small solinoid is added, attached with two capasitors. So it can shoot the puck when it finds itself at the right place.

RB is a regular two-wheel robot using ball caster as its third point. Its motors are 50:1 gear ratio high power Micro Metal Gearmotors, which make it crazy when commanded with 100% PWM.
